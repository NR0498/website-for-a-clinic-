Care philosophy

Health guidance should be understandable, steady, and personal.

My consultation approach begins with the patient's full story: symptoms, routine, sleep, diet, stress, previous reports, treatment history, and personal health goals. This helps create a care plan that is not limited to a single complaint.

At Arogyam Ayurved and Naturopathy Centre, the intent is to support prevention, recovery, and day-to-day wellness through Ayurved, naturopathy, lifestyle correction, diet guidance, and follow-up support.
